Interested in our business and its origins, hmm? Well, I can tell you with words.

Overview

Simply put, Website 2 Behold designs websites to behold. Founded in 2020, we are a new company that focuses on quality designs, because we know how important a good look is. The founder, Neville Pochara, who likes writing about himself in the 3rd person, had an idea one day. He always enjoyed the technical side of things (although he was no artist/designer. And yes this is still Neville writing, so I don't know why this is still in 3rd person) so he learned Hitimel. I'm sorry, I meant HTML. And CSS too. It was cool and stuff, so he decided to make a company based on it. By being a daily consumer of websites (you have no idea) Neville has a good idea of what people like in them. The website of a company or whoever is like their face on the Internet. You want a good look, especially among a bunch of other designers and websites. The quality rises above, which is what we aim for here. Look at me design the page right now!

History

Look, it's a free lance

We started in 2020 as a small freelance Web Design company. Since our (or at the time, my) first customer was happy with what they got and they spread our name to someone else. That someone else then spread our name further and pretty soon we (I) realized it'll get busy and hired new designers. Now we are what we are today, a cheap, quick, reliable, reputable, awesome, great, cool, amicable (oops, lost my thesaurus) company. We all enjoy what we do here, and do quality work to ensure we stand out well.
